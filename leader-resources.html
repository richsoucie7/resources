Last reviewed May 2026 · Lion through Arrow of Light

Two things to keep in mind. (1) The Cub Scout program was refreshed in June 2024; many adventure names changed. Always cross-check requirements against the official adventure library before delivering. (2) The organization rebranded to Scouting America in February 2025. Some older blog posts still say "BSA" or use the retired Cub Scout Promise / Law of the Pack — review and refresh that language before reading aloud.
