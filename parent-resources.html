New family checklist

  • Register your scout at beascout.scouting.org or talk with the Cubmaster about joining mid-year.
  • Create your my.Scouting account so you can be linked to your scout in Scoutbook.
  • Complete free Youth Protection Training (YPT) at my.Scouting — required for any registered adult, recommended for any parent involved at events.
  • Pick up the rank handbook (Lion, Tiger, Wolf, Bear, Webelos, or Arrow of Light) at the official Scout Shop or borrow one from the pack.
  • Get the basic uniform: rank-appropriate shirt, neckerchief and slide, hat, and Pack 828 council/numerals.
  • Save these emergency contacts: Cubmaster (above), and your scout's den leader.
  • Add yourself to the pack communication channel [Band, GroupMe, email list, etc.].

How to get help

Questions? Email cubmaster@pack828sc.org or talk to your den leader at the next meeting. We're a small pack — nothing is a silly question.

Two quick notes about the program. (1) The Cub Scout program was refreshed in June 2024, with renamed and restructured adventures. If you find an article that mentions an old adventure name, double-check it against the current rank handbook. (2) Boy Scouts of America rebranded to Scouting America in February 2025. You'll see both names around — same organization, just a new name.
